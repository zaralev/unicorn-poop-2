  • Movie Trailer

    For my Audio Production and Post final project, I took the Wallace & Gromit: Curse of the Were-Rabbit trailer by Aardman and Dreamworks Studios, and removed all original audio. I recorded my own ADR (thank you friends!) and foley, and used either free or paid for sound effects, as well as my own music choice. Project was created in Pro Tools.

  • Sound FX Design

    Original clip is from the movie Amélie. I removed all original sound and voiceovers, and used pre-recorded sound effects to create my own mix. All done in Pro Tools.

  • Test Framing Shots

    Shots for my Basic Shooting and Lighting course homework at Drexel University -- still shot with focus on interesting frame composition, movement, contrast, and lighting.

  • Stop Motion

    Final project for Basic Shooting and Lighting. Fun stop-motion animation short film, using theory learned throughout the course. Played around with pairing music to a moving image.
